Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the Cyber Security Club?

You can join our club by filling out the membership application form on our website or by attending one of our introductory sessions. Membership is open to all students of Far Western University with an interest in cybersecurity.

Do I need prior knowledge of cybersecurity to join?

No prior knowledge is required! Our club welcomes members of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced. We offer introductory workshops and resources to help newcomers get started in the field of cybersecurity.

How often does the club meet?

We typically hold general meetings twice a month, with additional workshops, training sessions, and events scheduled throughout the semester. Check our Events page for the latest schedule.

Can I contribute to the club's blog?

We encourage members to share their knowledge and insights through our blog. If you're interested in contributing, please contact our content team or submit your article through the form on our Blogs page.
